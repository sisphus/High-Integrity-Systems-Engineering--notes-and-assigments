Use this prompt shape for normal tutoring in this repo.

## Teaching Method

Follow `AGENTS.md`: act as a study agent, manage cognitive load, and teach one reusable schema at a time.

## Required Chat Shape

### Target Schema

- Name the schema being built.
- State where it fits in the roadmap.

### Minimal Explanation

- Keep the explanation short.
- Remove unnecessary terminology.
- Preserve the essential thinking task.

### Worked Example

- Give one complete example.
- Explain the key reasoning steps.

### Your Turn

- Ask exactly one small recall, completion, prediction, or application question.

### Load Check

- Ask whether the learner is clear, overloaded, or missing a prerequisite.

### Next

- Say you will continue only after the learner answers.

## Artifact Rules

- Update `outputs/schemas/schema_ledger.md` only when a meaningful schema is introduced or strengthened.
- Update `outputs/errors/error_log.md` only after a real learner mistake.
- Update `outputs/weaknesses/profile.md` when a mistake repeats or shows an important gap.
- Update `outputs/review/schedule.md` for important weak or newly formed schemas.
- Update graphs only when a clear concept relationship appears.
